# User Documentation

Welcome to the **IITG Affairs Portal** user guide. This portal is the central hub for academic management, hostel services, and administrative workflows at IIT Guwahati.

## Accessing the Portal
- Log in securely via the main portal page using your university credentials (User ID and Password).
- If you forget your password, click on "Forgot Password" to initiate an OTP-based password reset workflow.
- First-time users imported via CSV are required to change their default password upon their initial login.

## Role-Based Workflows

The portal automatically adapts to your designated role upon logging in. The four active roles are:
1. **Student**
2. **Faculty**
3. **Admin**
4. **HMC Member**

---

### 1. Student Portal

**Academic Management**
- **Dashboard**: View an aggregated overview of your academic standing, including upcoming assignments, pending tasks, and recent announcements.
- **Course Registration**: Browse the course catalog and enroll in offerings for the current semester. Note that enrollments have strict capacity limits that are protected system-wide.
- **Course Drop**: Drop courses directly from your portal if the current date is before the administrative drop deadline.
- **Assignments & Attendance**: Upload assignment files (PDFs, docs, etc.) directly to your course offering. Track your attendance session-by-session.

**Hostel & Welfare Services (HMC)**
- **Complaints**: Log maintenance or facility complaints and attach supporting images. Track the real-time resolution status of each complaint.
- **Leave Application**: Apply for hostel leave. Requests are routed to the HMC for approval.
- **No-Dues Clearance**: Initiate and track the automated clearance process across different departments prior to graduation.

**Documents**
- **Transcript Requests**: Request official academic transcripts. Approved requests can be downloaded instantly as dynamically generated, authenticated PDF files.

---

### 2. Faculty Portal

**Course Administration**
- **Dashboard Overview**: Monitor all the courses you are instructing this semester.
- **Attendance**: Manage daily attendance sheets. The system tracks absent/present flags per student per session.
- **Assignments**: Configure new assignments. Set strict deadlines and file-type restrictions. 
- **Grading & Submissions**: Review assignment submissions uploaded by students. Post grades and constructive feedback.
- **Final Grades**: At the end of the semester, finalize and submit final grades.

**Communication**
- **Announcements**: Broadcast messages instantly to all students enrolled in a specific course offering.
- **Resources**: Share lecture notes, syllabus files, and other educational materials.

---

### 3. Administrator Portal

**Institution Configuration**
- **System-Wide Parameters**: Control global settings, such as academic deadlines and credit minimums.
- **Catalog Management**: Add or remove Departments, Programs, and core Courses. Construct Course Offerings and assign instructors.

**User Management**
- **Faculty Directory**: Register new faculty members and update department mappings.
- **Student Directory & Bulk Import**: Manage the student body. The portal features an advanced CSV Bulk Import tool that allows administrators to upload spreadsheets of student data. The system automatically handles header mappings and dynamic data-type assignments.

**Oversight**
- **Enrollment Monitoring**: View real-time statistics on course capacities and student enrollment patterns.
- **Document Requests**: Review and approve/reject official transcript and certificate requests initiated by students.
- **Analytics**: Access global, department-level, and course-level metrics.

---

### 4. HMC Member Portal

**Hostel Management**
- **Leave Approvals**: Review pending leave applications submitted by students in your designated hostel. Approve or reject applications with comments.
- **Complaint Resolution**: Review hostel complaints, assign maintenance tasks, and update statuses (e.g., from "Pending" to "Resolved").
- **Asset Logging**: Keep track of hostel assets and schedule routine maintenance operations.

---

## Security & Best Practices
- **Logout Safely**: Always use the Logout button accessible via the sidebar to terminate your active session securely.
- **Password Security**: Maintain a strong password (8–16 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special symbols). Do not use your username as a substring.
